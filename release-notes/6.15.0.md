- **Discard HEAD Request For URL Tracking
  ([35328](https://github.com/civicrm/civicrm-core/pull/35328))**

  HEAD requests make up a significant portion of all click-tracking traffic,
  which are email security scanners and link previewers (e.g. Microsoft
  SafeLinks) and not real human clicks and should not be counted as url clicks,
  this improves the click through url count by ignoring HEAD requests for click
  through tracking.
